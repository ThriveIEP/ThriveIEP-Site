Our AI Tool Roadmap

Child Power Statement In Beta

"How do I introduce my child to his teacher? How do I let the teacher and school know that makes my child great, what makes him stressed, and what works?"

Our Solution: This tool draws on existing documents and insights from you to create a snapshot of you child that can be shared with your child's teacher and other staff on day one of school.

IEP Evaluator Planned

"How do I know if this IEP, which is 21 pages long, is actually right about my child? How do I understand the impact of their recommendations? Will it actually work?"

Our Solution: Our AI transforms your IEP into a digest, complete with actionable insights, and recommended changes and additions. It will help you communicate better with the school and understand priorities for your child. Perspective on a problem is the first step in finding solutions.

IEP Meeting Checklist Planned

"I am worried that I will not ask the right questions when I meet with my child's teacher."

Our Solution: Our AI tools build you a meeting checklist, based on your child's progress reports, evaluations, and other school documents. You go into that meeting ready to ask the right questions, in the right order. Prepared. Prepared.
